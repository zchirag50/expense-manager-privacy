Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 15, 2025

Thank you for using Expense Manager ("the App"). Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App may collect, how it is used, stored, and shared, and the choices you have regarding that information.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Data You Provide

The App allows you to create and manage financial records such as expenses, income, budgets, categories, and notes. This information is referred to in this policy as "Your Data."

1.2 Automatically Collected Data

We may collect non-personal technical information about your device and how you use the App, including:

  • Device model and operating system
  • Crash reports and performance data
  • App usage statistics (e.g., features used, session length)

2. How We Store Your Data

Local Storage: By default, Your Data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to us.

Cloud Sync: your data are auto sync and cloud backup. Your Data will be stored in Firebase services (or another cloud provider you choose). Cloud storage is encrypted by the cloud provider at rest and in transit.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • Provide core app functionality (display transactions, compute balances, budgets, reports).
  • Improve the App via analytics and crash reports.
  • Restore your data across devices if you opt into cloud sync.

4. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services to power features of the App. These services may collect limited non-personal information for analytics and diagnostics. Examples include:

  • Firebase (Authentication, Realtime Database / Firestore, Crashlytics, Analytics)
  • Google Play Services (for Play Store integration)

Third-party service providers have their own privacy policies; please review them for details.

5. Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer Your Data to outside parties except in the following limited cases:

  • When you explicitly request account or data export.
  • To comply with legal obligations or protect our rights.
  • When aggregated, non-identifiable analytics are shared to improve the App.

6. Data Retention & Deletion

You control your data. To delete your data:

  • Clear the App data from your device (Settings → Apps → Expense Manager → Clear data).
  • If you enabled cloud sync, sign in to the App and use the Delete Account / Delete Cloud Data option (if available), or contact us to request deletion.

7. Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your data. However, no method of transmission or storage can be guaranteed 100% secure. If you believe your data has been compromised, please contact us immediately.

8. Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child provided us with personal information, contact us to request deletion.

9. International Transfers

If you are located outside the country where the App developer operates, your information may be transferred to and processed in other countries. By using the App, you consent to such transfers.

10. Your Rights (GDPR & Other Jurisdictions)
