Data Deletion Request – Expense Manager

This page explains how you can request the deletion of your data collected by the Expense Manager mobile application. We take user privacy seriously and provide a simple way to remove all your stored information.

How Data Is Stored

Expense Manager stores your data in two possible ways:

  • Local Device Storage – If you are not using cloud sync, all your expense data is stored only on your device and is automatically deleted when you uninstall the app.
  • Firebase Cloud Storage – If you use cloud sync/login, your expense data is stored securely in Firebase (Google Cloud).

Request to Delete Cloud Data

If you have used cloud sync or logged into the app, you can request deletion of all your cloud-stored data.

Send an email request to:
Email: your-email@example.com

Please include the following details in your email:

  • Your registered email ID used in the app
  • Subject: Data Deletion Request – Expense Manager
  • A message requesting deletion of your account and associated data

Deletion Timeline

After receiving your request:

  • Your data will be permanently deleted from Firebase within 7 business days.
  • You will receive a confirmation email once the deletion process is complete.

Local Data Deletion

If you are not using cloud sync/login, all data is stored on your device only. To delete your local data:

  • Clear app data from device settings, or
  • Uninstall the Expense Manager app

Need More Help?

If you have any questions about data removal, feel free to contact us at the email above. We will assist you promptly.
