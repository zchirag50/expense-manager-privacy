Delete My Data – Expense Manager

Last Updated: 16-Nov-2025

This page explains how you can request deletion of your data stored in the Expense Manager app. We value user privacy and give full control over your data, whether it exists locally on your device or synced to Firebase Cloud.

1. Delete Data Stored Locally (On Your Device)

You can delete your local data anytime using one of the following methods:

  • Option 1: Open your device settings → Apps → Expense Manager → Clear Data
  • Option 2: Uninstalling the app will also delete all locally stored data

No request is needed because local data is fully controlled by you.

2. Delete Cloud Data (Firebase Sync)

If you enabled cloud sync in the app, your expense data, income history, and budgets may be stored in Firebase. You can request complete removal of this synced data.

To request deletion of cloud data, email us at:
your-email@example.com

Please include:

  • Your registered email (used for login or sync)
  • Subject line: Delete my Expense Manager data
  • Optional: Your user ID (if displayed in the app)

We will permanently delete your cloud data from Firebase within 48–72 hours and notify you once completed.

3. What Data Will Be Deleted?

Upon request, we will delete the following synced information:

  • Expense records
  • Income records
  • Budget settings
  • User profile (if any)
  • Any other cloud-synced financial data

Note: We cannot recover data once deletion is completed.
